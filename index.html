Some of my services

Below you can find some of the basic packages that I offer, but I am always willing to create a customized program that meets the specific needs of your pet. Some options are: custom walk times, training for specific behaviors, socializing with other animals, bath, nail trim, and more! Feel free to contact me and let me know what I can do for your furry friend!

20 minute walk

We'll take a quick stroll with plenty of stops for sniffs and bathroom breaks, along with a few treats and plenty of scratches and belly rubs!

45 minute walk

Basic dog training and reinforcement

Does your pup need a little help with their ABCs of obedience? We'll do positive reinforcement training together, based on an agreed upon plan that will help your dog learn how to listen and respond. All while having a great time!

Only available on weekends, this is my premium package for doggos that can go the extra mile… or ten!
